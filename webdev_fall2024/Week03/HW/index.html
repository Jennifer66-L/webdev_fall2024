Spaghetti Bolognese

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 45 minutes

Serves: 4 people

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 200g minced beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan and saut√© the onions and garlic until soft.
  2. Add the minced beef and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in the canned tomatoes and simmer for 30 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions.
  5. Drain the spaghetti and mix with the sauce.
  6. Serve with grated Parmesan on top if desired.
