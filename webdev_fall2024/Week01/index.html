How to Make a Avocado Toast

Creamy avocado spread onto well-toasted bread is one of my favorite quick meals. Actually, it’s one of my favorite meals in general. It’s so delicious, I’d call it one of life’s simplest pleasures.

  • Ingredients
    1. Avocados
    2. Breads
    3. Egg
    4. Sea Salt
    5. Herbs
  • Instructions
    1. Pick great avocados
    2. Buy good bread and toast it well
    3. Mash your avocado separately
    4. Don't forget the salt
